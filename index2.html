8fig works with online sellers in the United States and Canada (excluding Quebec). We can help you take your operations and growth to the next level if your store has been in operation for at least 12 months, you have an annual revenue of $100,000 or more, and your average monthly revenue is $8,000 for the last three months. Please note that 8fig does not work with Dropshipping sellers.

Unlike other solutions, 8fig will never presume to tell you how much funding you qualify for, since that is not how businesses grow. You’re the expert in your domain, so create an 8fig account and tell US how much capital you need and how it’ll be used. Our AI will then dive into the numbers to validate your ambitious goals and build your custom Growth Plan, complete with cash flow, revenue and other projections. If you can plan it, we will fund it.

The Cost of Capital for every Growth Plan is usually between $6,000-$10,000 for every $100,000 included in the Growth Plan.

8fig offers its AI funding platform and cash flow planning completely for free. Our Growth Plans are designed first and foremost to ensure that the hyper-growth ahead does not negatively impact business operations or the bottom line. To achieve this, 8fig leverages artificial intelligence and other technologies to assess the risk of each Growth Plan. This risk is represented by the cost of capital, a single transparent figure that reflects 8fig’s charge for each Growth Plan.
